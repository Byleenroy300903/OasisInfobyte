Top Anime List

  • Attack on Titan

    Several hundred years ago, humans were nearly exterminated by giants. Now, people live in peace behind city walls, but the threat of giants still looms.

  • Death Note

    Light Yagami finds a mysterious notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone whose name and face he knows. He decides to use it to create a perfect world.

  • One Punch Man

    Saitama is a hero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch, but he becomes bored with his lack of challenge and struggles to find a worthy opponent.

  • Naruto

    Naruto Uzumaki dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja in his village, but he must overcome many obstacles and enemies to achieve his goal.

  • Fullmetal Alchemist

    After attempting to resurrect their mother using alchemy, two brothers set out on a journey to find the Philosopher's Stone and restore their bodies.

  • My Hero Academia

    Izuku Midoriya is born without superpowers in a world where they are the norm, but he still dreams of becoming a hero and enrolls in a prestigious hero academy.
